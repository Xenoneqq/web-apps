Horror Rougelite Experience

Step into The Hallways, an endless maze in a world warped by shadows and danger. You’re trapped here, surrounded by twisting corridors and silent, empty rooms that pulse with a strange, unsettling energy. To survive, you’ll have to explore, uncover clues, and avoid the deadly entities that hunt anything that moves.

Dare to enter? Only the strongest will make it out.

What is Entity Zero?

Entity Zero is a horror roguelite that immerses you in the unsettling darkness of The Hallways, a labyrinthine world filled with deadly creatures and hidden dangers. Explore abandoned corridors, scavenge for supplies, and hunt down elusive exit keys to escape with whatever you can gather. As you move through this twisted maze, you’ll uncover clues that reveal the eerie secrets of this forsaken place and learn how to exploit each entity’s unique weaknesses to survive.

Allways something NEW!

Every level in Entity Zero is randomly generated, ensuring a fresh, tense experience with every run. Each floor holds a new mix of enemies, unpredictable room layouts, and randomized loot that forces you to adapt quickly to every situation. In this ever-changing nightmare, every choice counts
will you make it out alive?
